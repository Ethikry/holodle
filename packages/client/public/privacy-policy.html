Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Holodle (the "Service") collects, uses, and stores information when you use the Discord application, website, and related services available through holodle.win.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you interact with the Service, Holodle may collect:

  • Discord user IDs;
  • Discord server IDs;
  • Usernames and display names provided by Discord;
  • Basic usage and interaction data;
  • Error logs and diagnostic information.

2. How Information Is Used

Information is used solely for purposes related to operating, maintaining, and improving the Service, including:

  • Providing bot functionality;
  • Saving preferences or game progress;
  • Preventing abuse and maintaining security;
  • Debugging and performance monitoring.

3. Data Sharing

Holodle does not sell personal information. Information may only be shared when legally required or necessary to protect the security and integrity of the Service.

4. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as reasonably necessary for operation of the Service.

5. Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored information, though no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.
